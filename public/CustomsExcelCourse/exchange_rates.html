Currency Exchange Rates

The following list provides rates on goods commonly imported as accompanied baggage.

Note: Revenue Replacement Duty (RRD) is calculated as follow: Computed value based on (cost, insurance and freight or shipping (CIF value) + Import Duty) * RRD rate.

General Sales Tax is calculated as follow:
((CIF + Import Duty (ID) + RRD (where applicable))+ Environmental Tax) * 12.5%

Environmental Tax is calculated as 3% of the CIF value

 

RATE OF EXCHANGE TO BE USED TO CONVERT FOREIGN CURRENCY TO BELIZE DOLLAR

The rates listed below must be applied (multiplied) to the foreign currency stated on the invoice receipt, transport document and other proof of purchase documents in order to arrive at the customs value in Belize Currency.
